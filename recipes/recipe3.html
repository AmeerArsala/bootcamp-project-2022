Crunchy Blueberry Yakisoba

A wild card of a dish that defies tradition in order to go for a fresh, new taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving Yakisoba
  • A lot of blueberries
  • Handful of Granola
  • Curry Sauce
  • Garlic sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

Preparation

  1. Put olive oil into a pan and cook both eggs in there.
  2. After cooking, put them into a bowl to use as a base.
  3. Cook Yakisoba according to the packaging instructions. Put it in the bowl on top of the eggs.
  4. Put granola and blueberries on top.
  5. Put garlic and curry sauce on the dish.
