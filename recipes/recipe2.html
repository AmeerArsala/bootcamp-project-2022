Hearty Fiesta Sandwich

Oriental dish of legends with a varied history. It's like a party in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • 1 piece of sandwich bread
  • 1 chicken patty
  • 3 tbsp. Lean Ground Beef
  • 14 blueberries
  • Handful of fruity pebbles
  • 1 tbsp cream cheese
  • Cholula Chipotle Sauce
  • Pepper

Preparation

  1. Cut the sandwich bread in half.
  2. Microwave the chicken patty for 1.25 minutes.
  3. Cut the chicken patty into pieces.
  4. Cook and prepare the ground beef.
  5. Put the ground beef on one half of the bread and the chicken pieces on the other half.
  6. Put the fruity pebbles and blueberries in the sandwich.
  7. Put the chipotle sauce on.
  8. Glue it together with the cream cheese.
  9. Bon appetit!
