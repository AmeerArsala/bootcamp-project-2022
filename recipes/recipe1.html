Superfood Tomato Soup

Amazing soup filled with healthy superfoods that is sure to make any food connoisseur jump for joy.

Ingredients

  • 1 serving Tomato Basil Soup
  • ~3 pieces of steak
  • 1 Tuna&Veggie Patty
  • 10 blueberries
  • 2 tbsp. onion
  • 2 tbsp. cilantro
  • 2 tbsp. carrots and raisins
  • 2 tbsp. olive oil
  • Chipotle Black Pepper (spice)
  • Chipotle Tobasco Sauce
  • 1 tbsp. Cheddar Monterey Jack shredded cheese mix

Preparation

  1. Cook the steaks and prepare it by cutting them into pieces.
  2. Cook the Tuna&Veggie patty and prepare it by cutting it into pieces.
  3. Cook carrots and raisins together with olive oil on the stove.
  4. Cook the tomato basil soup in a bowl either by microwave or on the stove (according to package instructions).
  5. After getting to a good temperature, throw in the onions, cilantro, pieces of tuna&veggie patty, and pieces of steak.
  6. Put in the carrots and raisins.
  7. Sprinkle chipotle black pepper spice and put in chipotle tobasco sauce.
  8. Enjoy the masterpiece!
